# Sound Wave Music — Public Brand Usage Policy

This document explains where and how the Sound Wave Music brand may be referenced.

## Allowed Uses
You MAY:

• Refer to “Sound Wave Music” in commentary, review, news, educational content, or documentation.
• Share unmodified DJ mixes or visual works in non-commercial contexts with clear attribution.

Example attribution:
“Sound Wave Music — © 2025 Michael Widener — Shared under CC BY-NC-ND 4.0”

## Restricted Uses
You MAY NOT:

• Use the Sound Wave Music brand, name, or logo in a way that suggests endorsement.
• Rebrand, remix, alter, or re-upload modified audio or visuals.
• Use the brand or works in promotional, commercial, or paid environments.
• Sell merchandise, NFTs, albums, sample packs, or derivative content using the brand name.

## Prohibited Uses
You MAY NOT under any circumstances:

• Impersonate Sound Wave Music.
• Misrepresent association, partnership, or collaboration.
• Repackage or resell DJ mixes or musical content.
• Use the brand identity to promote or endorse third-party services.

About Sound Wave Music

Sound Wave Music features romantic, uplifting music mixed to create that special feeling we all recognize when life feels good. We don’t use gimmicks, shock value, or tracks with passive-aggressive or hostile lyrics. Every song is selected intentionally. We listen to every word, then play through entire playlists to ensure they maintain a consistent emotional tone. If a track disrupts the vibe, we switch it out. The goal is simple: good vibes only, from start to finish.

Notice: “Sound Wave Music” is a trade name/DBA used for marketing and does not denote a separate legal entity.

What Makes Our Sound Different

Sound Wave Music represents a new chapter. For many years, the name Phantom Records appeared around the world in entertainment circles—but over time, the name became associated with heavier, harder-edged genres and energy signatures that do not reflect our sound or purpose. While we appreciate the history, our mission now is different: we create warm, emotional, uplifting environments where people feel welcome, relaxed, and connected. Sound Wave Music is a deliberate shift into clarity—where music is chosen not for shock or intensity, but for how it feels in the room and in the heart.
